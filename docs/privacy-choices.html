Privacy Choices

You can manage your data and how the app uses it. Below is how to delete your account and how to keep your scores off the leaderboard.

Delete your account

Deleting your account removes your profile, friends and friend requests, and multiplayer game links from our servers. Your leaderboard entries may be kept for public rankings (as in our Privacy Policy). Your auth identity is removed so you can no longer sign in as that account.

How to delete your account in the app:

  • Open the app and go to the main menu.
  • Tap the gear icon (Settings).
  • Tap Delete Account.
  • Confirm in the dialog. The app will remove your account data and sign you out.

After deletion, on-device data (saved games, stats, preferences) stays on your device until you delete the app or clear its data.

Do not share scores to the leaderboard

You can choose to keep your scores private. When you do, the app does not upload your score, username, or seed to our servers for the public leaderboard. Your results stay on your device only.

How to keep scores private (or change it later):

  • Open the app and go to the main menu.
  • Tap the gear icon (Settings).
  • Tap Leaderboard Sharing → Manage.
  • Choose Keep Scores Private. Your choice is saved on the device.

The first time the app would submit a score (e.g. after a game), it may ask you to choose “Share Scores” or “Keep Scores Private.” You can change this later from the main menu as described above.
