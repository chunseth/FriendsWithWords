Support

If you need help with Friends With Words, please use the contact path below and include as much detail as possible.

Reasons to contact

  • User complaints — Report objectionable content, abusive behavior, or other concerns about another user’s conduct in multiplayer or public features.
  • Technical support — Bugs, crashes, or issues with the app.
  • Account or data — Questions about your account, saved games, or data.
  • Feedback — Suggestions or general feedback.
